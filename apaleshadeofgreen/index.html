Welcome to A Pale Shade Of Green, an eco living lifestyle website.

We know going green isn't easy and can't happen overnight, and we are not there yet, hence the name - a pale shade of green. As we embark on the journey to green living, we want to take others along with us, with helpful tips, product reviews and personal insights.
